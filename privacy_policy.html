Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

HealthifyDigital ("we", "our", or "us") built the TariffMate app as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

By using TariffMate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

TariffMate is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Calculation data — tariff calculations you perform are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • App preferences — your selected language and default currency are stored locally on your device.
  • Exchange rates — we fetch live currency exchange rates from the Frankfurter API (European Central Bank data). No personal data is sent during this request.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not store any data on our own servers.

4. In-App Purchases

TariffMate offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade via Google Play's in-app purchase system. All payment transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Frankfurter API (frankfurter.app) — provides live currency exchange rates sourced from the European Central Bank. No personal data is transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

TariffMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children.

7. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's own security mechanisms. We do not transmit personal data over the internet.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
